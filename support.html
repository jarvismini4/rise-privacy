Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your Rise Premium subscription at any time through your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Rise. Your premium access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Open Rise, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Clear All Data." This will permanently remove all your routines, progress history, and conversation data.
