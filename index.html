Rise - Recovery Coach

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

Rise (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) is a morning routine and recovery coaching app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data

Rise uses anonymous authentication. We do not require your name, email address, or password to use the app. A random anonymous identifier is generated when you first open the app.

Usage Data

We store the following data to provide app functionality:

  • Your selected routine preferences
  • Routine completion history (dates, streaks, feelings)
  • Subscription status (free or premium)

AI Conversation Data

Important — Third-Party AI Service Disclosure: When you use the AI coaching feature (“Talk to Rise”), your conversation messages are sent to Google’s Gemini API, a third-party artificial intelligence service operated by Google LLC, for processing and generating responses.

The following data is sent to Google’s Gemini API:

  • The text of your chat messages
  • Your conversation context (e.g., selected struggle topic, routine completion history summary)
  • The conversation trigger type (e.g., recovery, motivation)

We do not send your name, email, device identifiers, or any personally identifiable information to Google. Only your anonymous conversation content is transmitted.

Google’s use of data received through the Gemini API is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy and Gemini API Terms of Service.

Before using the AI coaching feature for the first time, the app will ask for your explicit consent to share your conversation data with Google’s Gemini API. You may use all other features of Rise without consenting to AI data sharing.

API Usage Data

We log the following metadata for each AI conversation to enforce rate limits and monitor service health:

  • Anonymous user ID
  • Conversation ID
  • Call type (conversation or quote)
  • Token counts (input/output) and estimated cost
  • Date and time of the request

This metadata does not include the content of your messages.

Purchase Data

If you subscribe to Rise Premium, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status through RevenueCat, our subscription management service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the app experience (routines, progress tracking, streaks)
  • To deliver AI coaching conversations via Google’s Gemini API
  • To manage your subscription status via RevenueCat
  • To enforce usage limits and prevent abuse
  • To improve the app and fix bugs

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored using Supabase, a secure cloud database service. Data is associated with your anonymous identifier only. We do not link your app data to any personally identifiable information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data is shared only with the following third-party services, each of which provides equivalent data protection:

Service Data Shared Purpose
Google Gemini API Conversation text, context AI coaching responses
RevenueCat Anonymous user ID, subscription status Subscription management
Apple App Store Payment info (handled by Apple) Payment processing
Supabase All app data (anonymous) Secure data storage

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings and tapping “Clear All Data.” This permanently removes all your routines, progress, and conversation history from our servers.

6. Children’s Privacy

Rise is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure database access controls, and server-side authentication for all API requests.

8. Your Rights

  • Delete all your data using the in-app “Clear All Data” feature
  • Use the app without providing any personal information
  • Decline AI data sharing and still use all non-AI features
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through the App Store

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
