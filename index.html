Rise - Recovery Coach

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 15, 2026

Rise ("we," "our," or "the app") is a morning routine and recovery coaching app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data: Rise uses anonymous authentication. We do not require your name, email address, or password to use the app. A random anonymous identifier is generated when you first open the app.

Usage Data: We store the following data to provide app functionality:

  • Your selected routine preferences
  • Routine completion history (dates, streaks, feelings)
  • AI conversation history within the app
  • Subscription status (free or premium)

AI Conversations: When you use the AI coaching feature, your messages are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. We do not store conversation content on our servers beyond what is needed to display your conversation history within the app. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Purchase Data: If you subscribe to Rise Premium, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status through RevenueCat, our subscription management service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the app experience (routines, progress tracking, streaks)
  • To deliver AI coaching conversations
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To improve the app and fix bugs

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored using Supabase, a secure cloud database service. Data is associated with your anonymous identifier only. We do not link your app data to any personally identifiable information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is only shared with:

  • Google Gemini API: To process AI coaching conversations
  • RevenueCat: To manage subscription status
  • Apple App Store: To process payments (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Supabase: To store app data securely

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you use the app. You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings and tapping "Clear All Data." This permanently removes all your routines, progress, and conversation history.

6. Children's Privacy

Rise is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and secure database access controls.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your data using the in-app "Clear All Data" feature
  • Use the app without providing any personal information
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through the App Store

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
